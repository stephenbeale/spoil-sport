# Chrome Web Store Listing — Spoil Sport

## Short Description (132 chars max)
Hides sports spoilers from YouTube. Blocks thumbnails and titles by keyword and channel so results stay hidden until you're ready.

## Detailed Description
Tired of having match results spoiled the moment you open YouTube? Spoil Sport keeps your feed spoiler-free by hiding sports videos before you see them.

When enabled, the extension scans your YouTube feed for sports content and replaces matching video thumbnails with a dark "Spoilers hidden" overlay, and rewrites titles to "Sports video (spoilers hidden)". You control what gets blocked.

HOW IT WORKS
- Keyword blocking: matches video titles against a configurable list of 40+ sports keywords covering football, rugby, cricket, tennis, F1, boxing, UFC, NFL, NBA, golf, Olympics, and more
- Channel blocking: hides all videos from known sports channels (Sky Sports, BT Sport, ESPN, BBC Sport, Premier League, etc.) regardless of title
- Instant toggle: turn blocking on or off from the popup with a single click
- Customisable: add or remove keywords and channels from the settings page to match exactly the sports you want to avoid

FEATURES
- Works on YouTube home feed, search results, sidebar recommendations, and subscriptions
- Adapts to YouTube's dynamic loading — new videos are caught as they appear
- Settings sync across your Chrome devices via Chrome Sync
- Lightweight — no external network calls, no tracking, no data collection
- Dark themed popup and settings UI
- Fully accessible: keyboard navigable, screen reader compatible, ARIA labels throughout

PRIVACY
Spoil Sport does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data. All settings are stored locally in Chrome Sync storage. The extension only runs on youtube.com and makes no external network requests.

PERFECT FOR
- Sports fans who record matches and watch later
- People who share a YouTube account or TV and want to avoid spoilers for others
- Anyone who doesn't follow sports and wants a cleaner YouTube feed

Spoil Sport is free, open source, and contains no ads.
